Sit Straight	A good posture makes you look more confident and professional
Avoid Slang	Slang is informal and you need to be as formal as possible
Be Polite	It never hurts to be nice!
Don't Figit or Fiddle with Things	It will make you look nervous and uncomfortable

Don't Curse	You definitely do not want your interviewer thinking you have a potty mouth
Ask Questions	This is very important, it shows your interviewer that you care and are genuinely interested in the job
